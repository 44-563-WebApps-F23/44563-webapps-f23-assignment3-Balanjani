St.Louis Arch

The Gateway Arch in St. Louis, Missouri, is a distinctive monument that stands on the west bank of the Mississippi River.It is known for its unique shape, which resembles a giant stainless steel arch. The arch is part of the Jefferson National Expansion Memorial, commemorating the westward expansion of the United States during the 19th century.
